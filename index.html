Marco Abbadini

I am a PostDoc Researcher in Mathematics at the University of Salerno (Italy).

Interests:

  • mathematical logic,
  • universal algebra,
  • duality theory,
  • ordered algebras (in particular: MV-algebras, lattice-ordered groups, vector lattices).

I am fascinated by the connections between logic, algebra, order and topology that emerged starting with M. H. Stone’s celebrated representation theorem for Boolean algebras in 1936. In my research I have focused on various Stone-like dualities and their applications, with a particular focus on MV-algebras and lattice-ordered groups.
