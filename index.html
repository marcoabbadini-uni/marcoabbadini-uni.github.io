Marco Abbadini Postdoctoral researcher

Marco Abbadini

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Université catholique de Louvain, Belgium.

My research lies at the interface of logic, algebra, order and topology, with a focus on Stone-like dualities and their applications to algebraic logic, MV-algebras, lattice-ordered groups, and related ordered structures.

CV.

  • Winner of the Ada Lettieri Prize (2025), awarded by the Italian Association for Logic and its Applications for the paper “Stone-Gelfand duality for metrically complete lattice-ordered groups”.
