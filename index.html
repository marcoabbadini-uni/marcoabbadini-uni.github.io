Interests:

  • mathematical logic,
  • universal algebra,
  • Stone-type dualities,
  • ordered algebras (in particular: MV-algebras, lattice-ordered groups, vector lattices).

I am interested in the connections between logic, algebra, order and topology that emerged starting with M. H. Stone’s celebrated representation theorem for Boolean algebras in 1936. The main topics of my research are Stone-like dualities and their applications, with a particular focus on MV-algebras and lattice-ordered groups.
